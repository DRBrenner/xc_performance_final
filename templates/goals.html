DO GOALS REALLY MATTER?

Per the graphs below, a very limited number of runners logged goals. The top two graphs make goals seem unimportant to success. Most races are won and PRs acheived without goals. And most goals that are set are not met. The bottom graphs focus on the runners who set goals and tell a different story. They show a positive correlation between goals and improved performance.
