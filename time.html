TIME AND GENDER

The graph below shows the average pace of every race's winner by gender per year. Although performance generally improves over time in most sports due to factors such as equipment improvements, training strategies, and nutrition; that doesn't appear to be the case in this dataset. Pace is relatively flat over the eight years of data. In contrast, gender is an important factor in race performance with the fastest men running about 1.3 minutes/mile faster than the fastest women.
